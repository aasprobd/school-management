About Global International

Learn about our history, mission, and vision for education

Our History

Established in 1995, Global International School & College began with a vision to provide quality education that bridges local values with global perspectives. What started as a small school with 200 students has now grown into a premier educational institution with over 3,000 students across both school and college divisions.

Over the years, we have consistently expanded our facilities, curriculum, and extracurricular offerings to meet the evolving needs of our students and the global community.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, moral integrity, and social responsibility, preparing students to become leaders and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of educational excellence that develops globally-minded citizens who contribute positively to society.

Admission Process

Admission Requirements

Admission to Global International School & College is competitive and based on academic achievement, entrance examination results, and personal interviews.

School Admission (Grade 1-12)

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school transcripts
  • Entrance examination in English and Mathematics
  • Interview with the admissions committee
  • Birth certificate copy

College Admission

  • Completed college application form
  • High school transcripts and certificates
  • Entrance examination in relevant subjects
  • Personal statement
  • Two letters of recommendation
